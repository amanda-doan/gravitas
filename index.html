Press <W> to jump.

Press <A> to go left.

Press <D> to go right.

s

Press <Spacebar> to freeze time.

While time is frozen:

  • Press <Left> to select the object to the left.
  • Press <Right> to select the object to the right.
  • Press <Up> to increase the gravity of the selected object.
  • Press <Down> to decrease the gravity of the selected object.
